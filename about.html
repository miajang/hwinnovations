Our Story

Who We Are

Health & Wellness Innovations (HWI) is a consulting and technology firm dedicated to building smarter systems for public health, pharmaceutical, and advocacy organizations. For over 25 years, we've been helping teams turn complex data into actionable insights and manual workflows into streamlined operations.

Our journey began with WATT™ (Wellness Anytime Through Technology) — a forward-thinking platform for chronic disease management and health education. Since then, we've expanded to deliver a full suite of digital solutions — custom applications, real-time dashboards, policy intelligence tools, and automation systems — all built for real-world impact.

Leadership

Meet the Founder

Mia Jang, PhD

Founder & President

With over 25 years of experience in healthcare innovation, health policy, and digital health, Mia Jang founded Health & Wellness Innovations to bridge the gap between technology and public health impact.

Her expertise spans obesity and metabolic disease, cardiovascular prevention, market access strategy, and executive decision support. She has built long-standing consulting relationships with leading pharmaceutical companies and government affairs teams.

Mia is a systems thinker who designs, builds, and delivers — from concept to deployment. Her work combines deep domain knowledge with hands-on technical capability, creating tools that serve real users in real environments.
